How to reach Goa from Andhra Pradesh ?

  • By Train : Direct trains are available from Guntur, Tirupathi to Madgoa (goa)
  • By Flight : Both direct and connected flights are available from all aiports of Andhra Pradesh

From airport you can react KTC bus stand panjim(panaji) by share/individual cabs.
If you come by train, if you stay is in south goa, get down at vasco-do-gama railway station else if it's North goa, them get down at Madgoan railway station. You should come to ktc bus stand panjim, because available the bike/car rentals are avaialble very nearby to that bus stand.

How to reach from Madgoan Railway station to KTC bus stand Panaji ?

  • Book a direct cab from railway station to ktc bus stand panaji at the prepaid cab booking counter outside railway station
  • (Cheapest way) Take a cab from raiway station to KADAMBA bus stand( 250rs per cab - 4 people), From KADAMBA to panjim bus stand , electric buses are available ( Fare: 35/-) and the journey is also very peaceful
